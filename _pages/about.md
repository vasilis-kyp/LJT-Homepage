## About Me

I am a first-year Ph.D. candidate at **HKUST NLP Group** (Hong Kong University of Science and Technology), supervised by **Professor Junxian He**. My research interests lie at the intersection of **Natural Language Processing** and **Machine Learning**.

I graduated from **Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)** in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.

### Research Interests

- **LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning**
- **Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)**
- **LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability**
